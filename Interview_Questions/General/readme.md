## Welcome to the General section of the Interview Questions folder
The **General** section covers interview questions that are applicable across various roles and industries.  These questions typically assess:
- **Problem-solving and logic:** Questions that challenge your approach to solving generic programming problems or logical puzzles.
- **Behavioral and soft skills:** Common HR and behavioral questions that test communication, teamwork, and adaptability in professional scenarios.
- **System design:** High-level questions related to designing scalable and efficient systems, often used in more senior-level interviews.
- **Algorithm and data structure fundamentals:** Broad questions on algorithms, data structures, and complexity analysis that apply to most coding interviews.

This section helps candidates prepare for the overall structure of interviews, no matter what role or company they're interviewing for.
